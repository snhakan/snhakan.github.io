About Us

Registered Add Xiamen
Type of business Spring, Wire forming, Stamping part
Main Market US, Europe, North America
Brand HS
Number of Employees 51-100 People
Annual sales US$2 Million - US$5 Million
Established in 2004

HongSheng is a growing manufacturer of spring, wire forming, stamping part and other metal components in past 16 years. We insist on doing what is our professional, what is our best to let our customer experience the best service. As an OEMs provider, our products have been widely used in various industries, such as Medical, Automotive, Electronic, Machinery etc,  

 

A supplier can't identify who we are, To be a partner with our customer is always we are pursuing. As we know customer is the most important part of us. Win-Wins is the essential of long lasting relationship. So we are committed to provide a comprehensive solution, rather than only selling.

 

We highly understand what our customer concerns, as we self often are a customer to our suppliers. So as a member of HongSheng, "Fast" has been deeply into our mind - Fast reply, fast action, fast delivery.


Also quality and price are the decisive elements, As a OEM manufacturer, supplying a competitive price with exquisite quality is what we are pursuing, So now, we are strictly with ourselves with the principle of Lean Production, improving production efficiency and reducing the waste. It's endless, and we will never stop.

 

To be or not to be? Of course we choose the first one, As the changing of times and technologies, so does us. We are constantly adapting alongside the evolving and ever-rising standards of the industries and customers that we serve.

 

Your business is ours.
